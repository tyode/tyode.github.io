BACKGROUND

Born and raised in the South bay of California, I grew up engulfed in a diverse community, surrounded by many hard-working and talented individuals who inspire me to strive for greatness in everything I do. While this "hustle culture" molded me to be a hard-worker and somewhat of a perfectionist, the following outlets help me to stay grounded and keep my mind healthy.
